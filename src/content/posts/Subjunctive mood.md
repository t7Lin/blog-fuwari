虚拟语气常用于表示：<font color =#FF2400>描述与事实相反的情况；不太可能发生的事情；提建议</font>
## If subordinate clause

| 虚拟时间维度  | If subordinate clause（条件）时态           | Main clause（结果）时态                       | eg.                                                                         |
| ------- | ------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| present | (simple past tense)**were /v.原形**     | **would/should/could/might + do**       | If I **were** you, I **would study** harder. (事实上我不是你)                      |
| past    | (past perfect tense)**had done**      | **would/should/could/might +have done** | If you **had listened** to me, you **wouldn't have failed**. (事实上去年没听，已经挂了) |
| future  | **v-ed/<br>were to do/<br>should do** | **would/should/could/might + do**       | If it **were to rain** tomorrow, I **would stay** home. (明天大概率不下雨)          |
_____
## If错综条件句

>主从句各自对应不同时间维度，并对相应的维度进行虚拟，从句（条件）过去，主句（结果）现在

**eg.**
- 1. If you **had taken** the medicine yesterday, you **would be** fine now.
   (昨天没吃药，导致现在难受)
- 2.If we **had booked** a table earlier,we **wouldn't be standing** here in a long queue now.

___
## wish/as if/if only

>愿望永远后退一步，<font color =#FF2400>时态倒退法</font>

| 情况        | 从句谓语形式                     | eg.                               |
| --------- | -------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| 现在无法实现的愿望 | **过去时 (did/were)**         | I wish I **were** taller.         |
| 过去无法改变的遗憾 | **过去完成时 (had done)**       | If only I **had studied** harder. |
| 将来不太可能的奢望 | **would/could/might + do** | I wish it **would stop** raining. |
- eg. He treats me **as if** I were a stranger. 
___
## **(should) do** in noun clauses

>凡是看到以下表<font color =#FF2400>坚持、命令、建议、要求</font>， that 从句谓语用<font color =#FF2400> (should) + do</font>。

<font color =#FF2400>insist，order, command，advise, suggest, recommend，propose,
ask, demand, require, request</font>

**eg.** The doctor suggested that he **(should) give up** smoking.
   
>**suggest** 表示**暗示、表明**时，**不用虚拟**！  

**eg.** Her pale face suggests that she **is** ill. (真实情况，用陈述语气)

>**<font color =#FF2400>It is +adj./n.+that</font>** ，从句谓语用 **<font color =#FF2400>（should）+do</font>**

- 1. **<font color =#FF2400>必须/应该做...</font>，主观，必要的重要的

- **<font color =#FF2400>essential, important, necessary, vital, strange, advisable</font>**

 **eg.** It is **essential** that he **(should) be** punctual. (他必须守时)

- 2. **<font color =#FF2400>竟然... ，客观，情绪/评价，惊讶、意外、怀疑、惋惜</font>**

- <font color =#FF2400>strange, surprising, astonishing, amazing,a pity, a shame, regrettable,disappointing</font>

 **eg.** It is strange that he **(should) know** nothing about it.

## Special cases

| 句式                                                             | 谓语要求                                                                                    | 例句                                            |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **<font color =#FF2400>It is (high) time that...</font>**该做... | **<font color =#FF2400>did / should do</font>** (不能用 would)                             | It's high time we **went** to bed.            |
| **<font color =#FF2400>Would rather that...</font>**宁愿         | 现在/将来用 **<font color =#FF2400>did</font>**；过去用 **<font color =#FF2400>had done</font>** | I'd rather you **hadn't told** him the truth. |
| **<font color =#FF2400>Without / But for...</font>**要不是...     | 主句用 **<font color =#FF2400>would/could have done</font>**                               | But for your help, I **would have failed**.   |
___
## Inversion and Ellipsis

**1. If 虚拟条件句的倒装  
><font color =#FF2400>省略 If，将 Were / Had / Should提至句首</font>。
- 原句：If I **were** you, ...
- 倒装：**Were** I you, ...
- 原句：If I **had** known, ...
- 倒装：**Had** I known, I would have told you.

**2. 含蓄虚拟**  
不用 If 从句，用逻辑词暗示虚拟条件。
> **<font color =#FF2400>事实（陈述语气）+otherwise+结果（虚拟语气）</font>**

- **eg.** I was ill that day. **Otherwise**, I **would have attended** the meeting.
  (Otherwise = If I hadn't been ill)
