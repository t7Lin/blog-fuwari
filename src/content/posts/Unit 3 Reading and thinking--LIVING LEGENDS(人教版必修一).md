# While-reading
## Skimming(2 min)
- Conclude the main idea of the text
## Scanning(5 min)
- 1. What made Lang Ping's determination be tested in the 2015 World Cup?
- 2. What does the first sentence in the first paragraph introducing Michael Jordan mean?
# Post-reading
- 1. Use a mind map to organize Lang Ping and Michael Jordan's main events from these aspects
  (sports,achievements,challenges,mental strength)
- 2. Decide what's a truth/an inference/an opinion(Page39.2)
- 3. What can we learn from this two Sports Living Legends?Why?
