# Pre-reading
Make predictions about the topic based on the posters and the content before reading.

# While-reading
## Skimming(2 min)
 - 1.Who have already been chosen as **the Living Legends** 
 - 2.Conclude the main idea of the text
## Scanning(5 min)
- 1.What made Lang Ping's determination be tested in the 2015 World Cup?
- 2.What does the first sentence in the first paragraph introducing Michael Jordan mean?
# Post-reading
- 1.Use a mind map to organize Lang Ping and Michael Jordan's main events
  (sports,achievements,challenges,mental strength)
- 2.Decide what's a truth/an inference/an opinion(Page39.2)
- 3.What can you learn from this two Sports Living Legends?Why?
# Summary
