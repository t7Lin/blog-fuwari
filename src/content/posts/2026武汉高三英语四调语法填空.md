In Gonghe, Qinghai Province, Yehdor the herder (牧民) ==carries== food and water while watching his sheep graze (吃草) across the summer landscape. Making the scene unique ==is== the blue sea of solar panels overhead, as the sheep’s grazing grounds 56. **are** (be) a photovoltaic (光伏的) industrial park.
  
The Talatan photovoltaic industrial park ==covers== 609 square kilometers and the flat land here ==enjoys== rich solar energy resources, with 57. **an** average annual sunshine duration of nearly 3,000 hours.

The park’s enormous collection of solar panels not only ==generates== electricity for distant provinces 58. **but** ==acts== as both a sunshade and a windbreak for the surrounding land. With average wind speeds 59. **reduced**(reduce) by half, vegetation coverage in the area ==has grown== to 80 percent.

However, this vegetation growth hasn’t been 60. **without** issues. The growing grass can 61. **dramatically**(dramatic) ==lower== panel efficiency and ==cause== fires in the dry winter months. To address this, the park ==invited== villagers to raise their sheep under the solar panels. The sheep ==fertilize== the area, allowing a green ecological chain 62. **to form** (form). In addition, herders ==can sell== some of their sheep, 63. **securing**(secure) great financial benefits. Last year, 600 sheep 64. **were tasked** (task) with protecting the solar panels in the park.

The photovoltaic industrial park ==has set== an example of 65. **how** industrial development, traditional practices, and environmental protection work hand in hand.

- 56.**主谓一致**；as引导原因状从，从句主系表结构，主语the sheep’s grazing grounds，grazing动名词表用途，中心名词grounds是复数形式，谓语与中心名词保持一致；主句也是主系表结构，倒装，强调这个光伏工业园景色之独特，Making the scene unique是现在分词短语做主语（语法角度）；头顶上空的如蓝海般的光伏嵌板使得这片光伏工业园的景色独特，因为这里还是喂羊的草地
- 57.**art.**;average annual sunshine duration首次出现泛指
- 58.**conj.**
- 59.**non-finite**;主句vegetation coverage... 80 percent, 谓语has grown;with引导状从，reduce是非谓语，主语average wind speeds是reduce这个动作的承受者，表被动，故用过去分词形式
- 60.**prep.**,语境理解，双重否定
- 61.**词性转换**;adv.修饰v.
- 62.**固定搭配**；allow...to do...;主句The sheep fertilize the area，谓语fertilize;allowing a green ecological...,现在分词短语引导结果状从
- 63.**non-finite**;主句herders...sheep,谓语can sell;状从...great financial benefits;此处考察secure（获得，实现）动词功能，在此是非谓语，整个主句是从句的主语，是secure这个动作的发出者，表主动，所以用现在分词形式
- 64.**passive voice**
- 65.**noun clause(object clause)**;主句The photovoltaic industrial park has set an example，谓语has set，此处of后面是名词性从句做of的宾语，因此后半句是一个宾从；宾从主句就是三个名词短语industrial development...，谓语是work，hand in hand描述一种方式,是方式状语，宾从不缺成分，需要一个连接副词使方式得以体现
