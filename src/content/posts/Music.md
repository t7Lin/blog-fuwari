>Music gave me happiness. When l listened to music, it made my spirits fly **like** a kite in the wind. Music gave me strength and brought me relief. It **was** the rock l leant on to become strong and to get through those hard times.Moreover, music gave me hope and a sense of satisfaction. lt **became** my best friend. lt **spoke** words of encouragement to the deepest part of my being.

## Pre-writing
structure repeat:(clear structure)
**Music gave me** happiness.  + examples
**Music gave me** strength and brought me relief. + example
Moreover, **music gave me** hope and a sense of satisfaction. + example

Rhetorical devices:(vivid description)
**simile**: ..., it made my spirits fly **like** a kite...
**metaphor**:It **was** the rock...;
lt **became** my best friend.
**Personification**: lt **spoke** words
