```
  
1.题目：A trip to Australia；

2.内容：

MIKE: Look, Scott. This is a photograph I took during my trip to Australia.

SCOTT: Let me see it, Mike.

SCOTT: This is a good photograph. Who are these people?

MIKE: They're people I met during the trip.

MIKE: That's the ship we traveled on.

SCOTT: What a beautiful ship!

SCOTT: Who's this?

MIKE: That's the man I told you about. Remember?

SCOTT: Ah yes. The one who offered you a job in Australia.

MIKE: That's right.

SCOTT: Who's this?

MIKE: Guess!

SCOTT: It's not you, is it?

MIKE: That's right.

MIKE: I grew a beard during the trip, but I shaved it off when I came home.

SCOTT: Why did you shave it off?

MIKE: My wife didn't like it!

3.基本要求：

（1）朗读文章；

（2）听力教学，设计帮助学生理解细节的教学活动；

（3）全英授课，适当设计板书
```

## Teaching objectives
**language ability:** master useful expressions when introducing photos to others
**learning ability:** learn expressions based on auditory materials
**thinking ability:** make details inferences based on listening materials
**cultural awareness:** foster interest through travel activity

## Lead in
T: Present photos taken during travel
S: Where/when is this photo taken; what's this bridge;is this teenage you?
T: Let me introduce to you,guys
## Pre-listening
T: What were we doing just now?
S:
T：Yeah, pay attention to these expressions.our friend Mike just come back 
from his travel and he took many photos as well. Make a prediction from the 
title, what will he do?
S: Introduce them to his friends.
T: Now,let's listen and check if you're correct.
## While-listening
T: First listening, what are they doing (gist/main idea)
T: Second listening, complete the table on the screen

| people             | what can we know about |
| ------------------ | ---------------------- |
| ship               |                        |
| the man            |                        |
| the man with beard |                        |
|                    |                        |
T:Why did Mike shave off his beard?

## Post-listening
Work in pairs,suppose that one of you just come home from travel and take photos,
another is your friend,make a conversation as Mike and Scott.Use good expressions
from their dialogue.(5 min)

## Summary and Homework
Listening strategies, extensive listening (grasp gist)and intensive listening
(make inference of details),good expressions.

- 1.Write a short paragraph about Mike's travel.
- 2.Find some photos and introduce to us on next class.
