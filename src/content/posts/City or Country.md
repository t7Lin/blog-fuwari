>I've been moving from one city to the next **because of** my dad's job. We started in Hong Kong, **then** moved to Beijing, **then** Singapore, **and then** San Francisco. Now we live in London.l love city life **because** it's full of opportunities for culture, recreation, education and entertainment.
In London,my home is very close to museums, the zoo, cinemas and community centres, **so** l have lots to do in my free time. **Also**, **as** l live close to public libraries and bookshops, l always have a good book to read. **And if** l'm ever bored.l can easily find a stable Internet connection.

## Lead in
Have you ever experienced moving from one place to another? 
Do you like your living environment?
What is your ideal living environment?
